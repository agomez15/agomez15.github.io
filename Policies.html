Speech to Text Notes Application Policies

Privacy Statement

This policy discusses how the Speech to Text Notes application collects and receives data in respect to the privacy of all users. This would include the areas of email address, phone number, name, address, etc. This policy was last modified on March 20, 2018. We reserve the right to modify the policies at any respective time without antecedent notice.

Use of Personal Data

Speech to Text Notes collects personal information when registering for the application download. Information is tracked and recorded when clients use respective services and is sent to our server logs from the mobile app. This would consist of the respective IP address and Speech to Text Notes cookies.

Non Disclosure of Personal information

Speech to Text Notes does not disclose any personal information gathered through the application to third parties. In addition, Speech to Text Notes does not sell any information regarding its users. In respect to the policy, information may be shared under the following:

  • If it is determined that it is necessary to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, fraud, or situations involving potential threats to the safety of any individual.
  • If it is determined it is necessary to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding situations that involve abuse of the application infrastructure, any telecommunications service in general, or the Internet in general.
  • If Speech to Text Notes is acquired by or merged with a separate party.
  • In retaliation to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish, protect, or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims or demands.

    • Permissions

    The following are the permissions used in Speech to Text Notes as well as a detail of what the permission consists of.

  • RECORD_AUDIO - The application can properly record and compile to .wav.
  • INTERNET – API can log the results to designated storage.
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE - This permission allows the application to read and write to a SD card.

    • Children

    Speech to Text Notes is available through vendors to be downloaded by adults, however it still may be used by children. If the respective user is under 18, the application may be used under the supervision of a guardian or parent.

    Cookies

    Cookies are used to help provide services to our clients to ensure a reliable service.

    Third Party Services
